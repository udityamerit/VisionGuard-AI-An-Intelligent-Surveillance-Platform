AI Modules
Comprehensive Threat Intelligence
Seven specialized AI modules working in real-time to ensure complete situational awareness across every camera feed.
Weapon Detection

Real-time identification of firearms, knives, and other weapons using high-confidence YOLOv11 inference with immediate HIGH-PRIORITY alerts.

Crowd Density Analysis

Estimates real-time crowd count and density classification (Low / Medium / High) with automatic overcrowding alerts when thresholds are exceeded.

Face Verification

DeepFace-powered identity recognition distinguishes authorized personnel from unrecognized individuals, flagging unknown faces instantly.

Fight / Violence Detection

Tracks body proximity and velocity vectors of multiple persons to detect physical altercations and violent confrontations in real-time.

Anomaly Detection

Behavioral pattern engine that continuously evaluates combined signals — identifying composite threat scenarios that no single detector catches alone.

Object Tracking (SORT)

Persistent identity assignment across frames using the SORT algorithm — ensuring tracked subjects maintain unique IDs throughout video sequences.

Filter Lab — Vision Processing

7-algorithm visual processing toolkit: Black & White, Heat Signature, Canny Edge, Sketch, Cartoon, Emboss, Negative — with full video export.

Automated Alert Engine

Three-tier priority alert system (High / Medium / Low) with screenshot capture, CSV logging, and real-time dashboard notifications.
